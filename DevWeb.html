Programmers of Philadelphia

We are a group that meets in the basements of libraries of Philadelphia. The Clueless Coders combined with over 30 years experience, (and as little as 4 months experience) Are all together for a common good. To make learning to code and improving one's programming capabilities to the next level. We thrive on hard work and perserverance; and most of all common sense. All are welcome to our group who have the willingness and ability to not only survive in Software Development-- yet to thrive to our ultimate potential. We all have the same 24 hours in a day. It is up to us as individuals to determine what type of life we want to live.

This website is being created by Anderson and Matt. Anderson created at Meetup group called Aspiring Self-Taught Software Developers in September 2019. Anderson and Matt met during a meetup and they and a few other aspiring programmers decided to branch off and make another programming group called Clueless Programmers. This group meets twice a month and has about five other members. Anderson and Matt decided to build a website for our groups on the suggestion of one of our mentors within the Clueless group, Will.
